Fishbowl

What is life? Perhaps it’s a long road full of uncertainties and dwindling trails; it could be a vast ocean, waiting for you to venture far into the beyond; or it could be a box. A box filled with surprises. The Seville on Roxy is literally like a box. A box squished with people living their everyday lives. The book gives readers a peek at the lives of residents in the building. Connor, who owns a gold fish named Ian, was cheating on Katie with Faye and Deb. After doing so, he realized that his only love was Katie, but it was too late. Katie found out. Devastated, she left and they broke up. Petunia Delilah, on the other hand, is about to give birth to a baby. But there’s nobody home and her phone is out of battery. Meanwhile, Claire, staying her clean home and never leaving, had just lost her job. Herman, a boy is dealing with the fact that his only relative, grandpa passed away. Passed out on the hallway, he was found by Delilah searching for help. Claire helps by calling 911, and Herman helps deliver the baby. Unexpectedly, Claire find a new job and a date with the man on the other side of the phone. Jimenez, the lonely maintenance in the building faces a challenge: fixing the elevator. He’s done a poor job, but it managed to work. Garth, another lonely soul, has a secret. He likes wearing dresses, the feeling of expensive fabric sliding against his skin, how it floats and dances when he spins, it makes him feel beautiful. Jimenez, sent to fix his skin finds out this secret. The two talks and a warm feeling, perhaps the feeling of understanding, acceptance, rises. Lastly, Ian the goldfish leaves his fishbowl and goes on an adventure, plummeting down the building, and luckily, landing in Faye’s water bottle. In a short 30 minutes, we have witnessed the birth of a new live, it’s heart pounding as it lays in Herman’s arms; we have witnessed the heartbreaks of two people, both crying and regretting their choices; we have witnessed the start of a relationship of two lovely men, dancing and relaxing; we have witnessed the short fall of Ian, finally free from his fishbowl out for an adventure. People in this building has been through things, no matter good or bad. It happens, like a programmed robot finishing one step then starting another. No turning back, all we have to do is just take a step forward. Just like how Ian took a leap of out his fishbowl, it’s time for all of us to take a leap of faith. Take a step forward, down the road; hoist the sails and explore, see what adventure awaits; or perhaps just start by choosing what to have for dinner.

蛤蟆先生去看心理医生 （Counselling for Toads）

故事的开始，蛤蟆先生抑郁了。他开始酗酒，失眠，变得十分邋遢。于是他的朋友们将他带到了心理医生那里去咨询。与苍鹭先生初次相识，他十分抑郁，给他自己的状态打了两分。但通过一次又一次与苍鹭的讨论，蛤蟆感悟到了自己为什么成为了自己的原因，变得活力满满。不仅如此，他也带领着我一起成长，解了到了我们的不同状态，人生中的不同坐标，增强了对自己的认识。
